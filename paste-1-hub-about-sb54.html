SB 54 in California

California is taking on one of its most ambitious recycling and packaging challenges yet. SB 54 is one of the most comprehensive and far-reaching packaging responsibility laws in the U.S., creating a producer-funded system to support recycling, composting, and waste management improvements across the state.

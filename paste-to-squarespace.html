Welcome

This is your starting point for what's ahead under SB 54 and how you can get involved.

California's recycling, composting and reuse system is evolving. Starting now and in the coming years, SB 54 will be driving real improvements in how single-use packaging and food serviceware is collected, processed, and recycled or returned for use – creating a more consistent, connected system statewide.

Whether you've already connected with CAA or are learning about CAA for the first time, this page is designed to help you understand what's coming next and how you can engage.
